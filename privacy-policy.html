Introduction

Welcome to TeamoTrack ("we", "our", "us"). TeamoTrack is a workforce management platform designed to help businesses manage employee attendance, GPS tracking, task management, leave management, reporting and other workforce operations.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and protect your personal information when you use our website, mobile applications, and related services.

By using TeamoTrack, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services.

👤

Personal Information

📍

Location Information

GPS location may be collected when attendance, tracking or geofencing features are enabled.

📱

Device model, operating system, application version, language and device identifiers.

📊

Usage Information

Attendance, tasks, reports, login activity and feature usage.

LOCATION DATA

How We Use Location Information

TeamoTrack uses location information only for workforce management purposes.

Location information may be collected to verify attendance, track field employees, record travel history, support geofencing and generate work reports.

Location collection depends on your employer's configuration and your granted permissions.
